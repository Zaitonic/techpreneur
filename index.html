About CDM Eats

Discover our mission to fuel student success with tasty, affordable meals

We're Here to Serve Students

CDM Eats was founded with one simple mission: to provide affordable, delicious, and nutritious meals to the students of Colegio de Montalban.

We understand the challenges students face when it comes to budgeting for food while focusing on their studies. That's why we've created a menu that offers great value without compromising on taste or quality.

Our team is committed to serving meals that not only satisfy hunger but also provide the energy needed for a full day of classes and activities.

Find us at the Colegio de Montalban Campus, Rodriguez, Rizal. We offer walk-in service only for easy access to all students.
